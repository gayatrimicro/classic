25 YEARS OF MANUFACTURING EXCELLENCE


Classic Metallic Sheets Factory LLC is a manufacturer of premium quality fasteners in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates (U.A.E). Our manufacturing plant is ISO 9001-2008 certified and consists of forging and rolling machines as well as a continuous heat treatment furnace. We have various in-house coating facilities such as hot dip galvanizing, zinc plating, fluoropolymer and phosphate coating. We are and always strive to be your trusted source for anchor bolts, stud bolts, drywall screws (gypsum screws), stainless steel and high tensile fasteners.
