Company Profile

Classic Metallic Sheet Factory LLC (CMSF), founded in 1992 by Mr. Shabbir A. Shaikh, is the leading manufacturer of fasteners in the Middle East. The company is based in Sharjah, UAE and comprises of 4 manufacturing divisions:

  • Anchor Bolts
  • Stud Bolts
  • Hex Bolts
  • Drywall Screws

CMSF is an ISO 9001-2008 certified manufacturer of Anchor Bolts, Hex Bolts, U- Bolts, Stud Bolts and Drywall Screws with in-house Hot Dip Galvanizing, Zinc Plating and Fluoropolymer Coating facilities as well as an in-house quality control testing facility.

CMSF facility spans over an area of 12,000 Sq. Meter and comprises of a total workforce of 180 members.
